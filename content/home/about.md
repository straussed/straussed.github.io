I am interested in the ecological and evolutionary forces affecting social systems. In particular, I'm interested how the interplay between cooperative and competitive forces within groups leads to the emergence of complex social structures such as dominance hierarchies. My current research on the structure of inequality in nepotistic societies in spotted hyenas focuses on three aspects of these societies - the ontogeny of hierarchies among juveniles, rank reversals and status dynamics among adults, and the stability of social and dominance structures at the group level.
