I am a behavioral ecologist interested in the ecology and evolution of complex social systems. In particular, I'm interested in the ecological, behavioral, and systems-level forces that produce social inequality in domains like health, social status, and social connectedness. My current research focus is on developing quantitative approaches to measuring hierarchical social structure and using them to study social inequality in the nepotistic societies of spotted hyenas.
